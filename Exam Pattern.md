# üìù Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert (AZ-305) - Exam Pattern

The **Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert (AZ-305)** certification validates your ability to design and implement **secure, scalable, and robust cloud solutions** on Azure. This document provides a detailed overview of the **exam pattern, domains, skills measured, preparation strategies, and professional benefits**.

---

## üìå Exam Overview
- **Exam Name:** Designing Microsoft Azure Infrastructure Solutions  
- **Exam Code:** AZ-305  
- **Duration:** 150 minutes  
- **Format:** Multiple-choice, case studies, drag-and-drop, scenario-based questions  
- **Passing Score:** 700/1000 points  
- **Exam Mode:** Online proctored or Pearson VUE testing center  
- **Languages:** English (others may be available)  

---

## üß© Exam Domains & Weightage

| Domain | Key Topics | Approx. Weight |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **1. Design Identity, Governance, and Monitoring Solutions** | Azure AD, RBAC, subscriptions, policies, governance strategies, monitoring solutions, cost management | 25-30% |
| **2. Design Data Storage Solutions** | Azure SQL, Cosmos DB, Blob Storage, Data Lakes, backup & recovery, data security, compliance | 15-20% |
| **3. Design Business Continuity** | High availability, disaster recovery, backup strategies, failover plans, RTO & RPO planning | 10-15% |
| **4. Design Infrastructure Solutions** | Compute solutions (VMs, AKS, App Services), networking (VNet, Load Balancers, VPN), hybrid integration, microservices architecture | 30-35% |
| **5. Design Security Solutions** | Identity & access management, encryption, key vaults, secure workloads, compliance with ISO/GDPR standards | 15-20% |

## üõ† Skills Measured
Candidates are expected to demonstrate the ability to:

- Plan and implement identity and access management solutions  
- Design governance strategies and monitor Azure resources effectively  
- Architect compute, storage, and network solutions in alignment with business requirements  
- Implement disaster recovery and high availability solutions  
- Ensure security and compliance across Azure workloads  
- Optimize cost, performance, and reliability in solution designs

## üèÜ Professional Benefits of Certification
1. **Career Growth:** Positions you for senior cloud roles such as Azure Solutions Architect, Cloud Consultant, or Cloud Infrastructure Lead.

3. **Strategic Influence:** Gain the authority to make design decisions, lead architecture reviews, and implement enterprise-scale solutions.  
4. **Recognition:** Stand out to recruiters and employers with a **globally recognized Azure certification**.

6. **Consulting Advantage:** Required for consulting positions and enterprise cloud projects.

## üìù Preparation Tips
- **Hands-On Labs:** Practice in the Azure Portal for real-world experience.  
- **Microsoft Learn:** Complete official learning paths tailored for AZ-305.  
- **Practice Exams:** Attempt full-length exams to familiarize with scenario-based questions.  
- **Case Studies:** Study real enterprise architectures to understand practical implementations.  
- **Well-Architected Framework:** Review the pillars: Cost, Performance, Reliability, Security, Operational Excellence.  
- **Exam Strategy:** Allocate time per domain, focus on weak areas, and read questions carefully for scenario details.
